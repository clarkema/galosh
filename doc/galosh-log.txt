galosh-log(1)
=============

NAME
----
galosh-log - Manage your amateur radio log

SYNOPSIS
--------
`galosh log` [<options>]

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Manage your amateur radio log.

`galosh log` is the heart of the Galosh suite; it allows you to quickly
and easily create new log entries, with as much or as little detail as is
appropriate.

OPTIONS
-------
--complete-missing-entities::
    Fill in DXCC entity information for any entries in your log that lack it.
